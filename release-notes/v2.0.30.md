## What's changed

- Fixed banned-account cleanup so SwiftTunnel switches off its custom Windows power plan and removes it even after an app restart.
- Prevented saved boost settings from reapplying the SwiftTunnel power plan after an account is banned.
- Kept power-plan cleanup conservative: SwiftTunnel only deletes its own exact plan GUID and falls back to Balanced before deleting an active plan.
