## What's changed

- Fixed Route Assist and Vietnam partial-bypass sessions losing Roblox menus/chat/player icons or failing after working temporarily. Route Assist now keeps gameplay assets, chat, avatar, and menu traffic direct while relaying only the Roblox join/control path; Partial Bypass also wins over Route Assist so the two modes do not overlap.

- Improved Roblox join/API reliability on unstable networks by avoiding relayed TCP packets that some ISPs silently drop.

- Improved startup and disconnect internet recovery. SwiftTunnel now clears leftover WinpkFilter adapter bindings while disconnected so PCs do not get stuck needing SwiftTunnel connected for normal internet.

- Kept Full Country Ban behavior separate for fully blocked networks, so stricter networks can still relay everything when needed.
