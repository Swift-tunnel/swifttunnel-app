## What's changed

- Fixed Roblox and Roblox Studio installs/updates failing to download their settings ("Continue installation without flag settings…") while connected with Route Assist. The launch-critical Roblox settings hosts now reliably stay off the relay: their addresses get an extra resolution retry at connect time, and if one is still unknown, SwiftTunnel recognizes it from the connection itself and routes the installer's next attempt directly.
- Bypass country bans mode is unaffected and still routes those hosts through the relay on purpose.

**Release**: https://github.com/Swift-tunnel/swifttunnel-app/releases/tag/v2.2.1
