## What's changed

- Fixed Roblox Route Assist breaking Roblox critical-settings downloads after connecting through the tunnel.
- Route Assist now only tunnels Roblox browser HTTP traffic when SwiftTunnel captures the TCP handshake and can apply the correct MSS clamp, preventing already-open Roblox HTTPS sessions from being moved into the relay midstream.
- Improved Route Assist TCP teardown handling so Roblox login, bootstrap, and account-switch traffic stays routed cleanly through disconnects, half-closed sessions, and recycled ports.

**Release**: https://github.com/Swift-tunnel/swifttunnel-app/releases/tag/v2.0.37
