## What's changed

- Fixed a bug where force-closing or crashing SwiftTunnel mid-session could leave your PC with no internet until a reboot. The app now detects this on its next launch and automatically restores your connection.
- Added an "Internet recovery" repair to the Repair Center that fixes no-internet-after-SwiftTunnel issues in one click: it clears stuck packet-filter state, removes leftover IPv6 blocks and adapter settings from previous sessions, and flushes the DNS cache.
- Disconnecting now verifies that your network adapters were fully released instead of assuming it.

**Release**: https://github.com/Swift-tunnel/swifttunnel-app/releases/tag/v2.2.2
