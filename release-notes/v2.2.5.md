## What's changed

· New in-game overlay: a movable on-screen overlay that shows live FPS, CPU, RAM, play time and more while you game, drawn over the screen without ever touching the game (anti-cheat safe). FPS is read straight from Windows. Just hover the bar in-game and hold-drag it wherever you want.

· Auto-clean RAM on game launch overlay: frees standby memory the moment you join a game and shows a "RAM freed" overlay.

· Fixed Roblox Route Assist no longer placing you in game servers near your tunneled region (and games not showing up in search). A recent change could let matchmaking and search slip onto your real connection on Roblox's shared servers — Route Assist now keeps that traffic on the relay while still loading textures and assets over your fast direct connection.

· Bypass is now two clear options. "Bypass Country Ban" is for when all of Roblox is blocked in your country (e.g. Egypt) — it relays everything and adds DPI evasion. "Bypass Partial Ban" is for when only specific games are blocked (e.g. TSB/JJS in Vietnam) — it relays just enough to find and join the game while keeping your real in-game ping.

· Better support for fully-blocked countries: added more secure-DNS fallbacks so Roblox and Roblox Studio can still fetch what they need when the usual ones are blocked, and the app now tells you when DNS repair couldn't get through.
