## What's changed

- Bypass Country Bans now keeps Roblox gameplay UDP direct unless Route Assist is also enabled, so specific-game-ban users can keep their normal in-game ping.
- Route Assist remains the full relay fallback when a stricter network block needs gameplay traffic relayed too.
- Added raw Roblox CDN hosts (`c0.rbxcdn.com` through `c7.rbxcdn.com`) to the country-ban asset split so reachable cutscene/assets stay direct and blocked ones can fall back through the relay.

- New Optimization features
