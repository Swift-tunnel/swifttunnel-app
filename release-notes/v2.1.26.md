## What's changed

**Fixes**
- Fixed "Bypass Country Bans" for fully blocked regions. It now routes all Roblox traffic - including the launch-critical settings - through the relay, so players in countries where Roblox is completely blocked (IP-level blocks) can launch and play again. It still runs the GoodbyeDPI helper on top for DPI/SNI-based blocks.
- Enabling "Bypass Country Bans" now automatically turns on Route Assist, so a single toggle is enough for blocked regions.
- Route Assist is unchanged for specific-game bans: critical Roblox hosts stay direct for reliability, and only game traffic is routed through the relay.

**Notes**
- Use Route Assist when only certain games are blocked in your country.
- Use Bypass Country Bans when Roblox itself is blocked in your country.
