## What's changed

- Added a separate **Bypass country bans** control that starts a scoped GoodbyeDPI helper for Roblox hostnames.
- Bypass country bans now applies to both browser launch/login traffic and the Roblox app without changing Route Assist relay ownership.
- Route Assist no longer routes browser-owned Roblox traffic, and uninstall cleanup now removes SwiftTunnel's GoodbyeDPI runtime files and stops only SwiftTunnel-managed GoodbyeDPI processes.

**Release**: https://github.com/Swift-tunnel/swifttunnel-app/releases/tag/v2.1.9
