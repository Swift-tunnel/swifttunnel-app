## What's changed

- Fixed Roblox split-tunnel process detection so SwiftTunnel only tunnels the standard Win32 Roblox player and studio executables.
- Stopped legacy or stale Microsoft Store Roblox entries from being treated as tunnelable, including the generic `Windows10Universal.exe` Store app host.
- Prevented substring lookalikes such as `window.exe` or `player.exe` from being mistaken for Roblox, reducing cases where the app could show connected while the actual game traffic was not selected.

**Release**: https://github.com/Swift-tunnel/swifttunnel-app/releases/tag/v2.0.14
